Learning to code in HTML

Seeing through the web:

  • View source in Chrome: Right click or opt+cmd+u.
  • View source in Safari: Preferences->Advanced->Show Developer Menu; Right click or opt+cmd+u.
  • View source in Firefox: Mac: cmd+u. Win: cntrl+u
  • View source in Windows Explorer:Win: cntrl+u
  • Can also add "view-source:" to url in Chrome or Safari
To view your code like a developer, we recommend Hackasaurus and Firebug.

Assignment #1: Autobiographical Page Experiment

In this assignment, you will create a single web page--a prototype of a home page for a personal web site. You will experiment with this page using autobiographical details -- related to your professional aspirations, extracurricular activities, hobbies, etc. -- to create this page. Think about how to best represent who you are using a combination of text, images, links, and related media forms such as audio and video. Also think about the spatial arrangement of your content. What is the most effective way and which is the most suitable medium format to display aspects of your identity to your audience? Who is your audience?
