Assignment #4: Build your site using a CMS or Framework

Select a CMS for your personal site from the list below. Think about how you want to manage and develop your content. Pick a theme and play with the layout of the site. Populate the site using content that you already have. Invite a friend to be a co-contributor to your site.

CMS List

  1. WordPress
  2. Omeka
  3. Drupal Gardens
  4. Tumblr
  5. Blogger

On our course site (to be shared), blog to reflect on your experiences of using the CMS. Compare your experiences of site-building between using the CMS and hand-coding. Do you find experience of using a CMS empowering or disempowering? Can you achieve your design goals — in terms of functionality and aesthetics — by using a CMS? How would the use of a CMS change your visions of audience? What are some of the limitations and drawbacks of each method? Discuss your preference in light the advantages and disadvantages of both system. Make sure that you link to your new site in your post so we can see it.
